MATRICULATION!!!

Congratulations to the newly matriculated students. This is a memorable day to rejoice and celebrate for the great privilege granted by the designer of heaven and earth to be among the students of the university, as numerous individuals are looking forward to seeking opportunity of this kind.
This ceremony marks the official entry of every students into the higher institution of learning and it is a statutory gathering to finally admit the deserving and qualified candidates who are fortunate to be offered admission into this prestigious university where academic excellence with Godliness and core value is being enforced. Today's Matriculation ceremony is very significant because it provides opportunity for students to pledge their allegiance to the authority of the university through the administration of Matriculation oath.
As part of the matriculated significant, every student is entitled to all right and privilege recognzed by the school authority which include but not limited to; right to access the information in the library, enjoyment of all teaching and learning facilities like I.C.T, participation in sport activities such as football, basket ball, table tennis e.t.c as well as the right to report any criminal activity to the cheif security officer.
